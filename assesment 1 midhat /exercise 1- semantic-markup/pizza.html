Homemade Pizza

Delicious pizza with fresh toppings made at home!

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. In a large bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  3. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rise in a warm place for 1 hour.
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  6. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, then sprinkle cheese and add toppings.
  7. Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  8. Slice and serve hot!
